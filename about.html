Our Mission

Every year, we bring together talented students and seasoned backend developers passionate about their craft. We create an environment that encourages collaboration, learning, and innovation in the realm of backend API development.

What We Do

Our community serves as a hub for discussions, workshops, and hackathons where individuals immerse themselves in the intricacies of backend development. We believe in honing skills, exchanging ideas, and collectively working towards building robust portfolios that showcase the power and creativity of backend APIs.

Join Us

Whether you're a novice eager to learn or an experienced backend developer seeking a vibrant community, we invite you to join our gatherings. Together, let's explore the world of backend API development, share insights, and create solutions that push the boundaries of technology.
